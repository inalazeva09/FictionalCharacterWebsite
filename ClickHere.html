Fun Facts about Winnie the Pooh:

1. Winnie was named after an actual real life black bear from Canada who lived in the London Zoo.

2. The "pooh" part of his name orginially came from a swan.

3. Winnie the Pooh is almost 104 years old.

4. Pooh's first story was published on October 14, 1926.

5. In 1961, Walt Disney Productions adapted the Winnie the Pooh stories.
